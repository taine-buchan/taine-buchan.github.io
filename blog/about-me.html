Hey, thanks for stopping by.


I'm Taine from West Auckland, I've explored various interests over the last 8ish years. I'm attracted to a challenge and love exploring new interests. I spent a few years in music, focusing on songwriting and production. I dabbled in the YouTube space eventually opening for one of my favorite musicians. Straight out of School I was desparate for coin! I went all in on a diploma in IT and Security Systems from Ames, then found a role in support for ESET Security. Needing to flex my creative bones, I decided I wanted to be an art director so I got my bachelor's degree in Communications majoring in Advertising. I spent three years whipping up social content for various clients such as Maccas, Woolworths, Sky, and Frucor brands.


Eventually I realised that advertising didn’t fully satisfy me, and I was seeking a new challenge that better aligned with my personality. After talking with a few friends about their experiences learning to code, Dev Academy caught my attention and so here I am!


Persistence helps me stay focused and committed, even when the solution isn't immediately clear. For me it's about being patient, finding different approaches, and continually trying. I have a few close friends who have been working in web development for some time, and their passion has rubbed off on me I guess! I'm excited to be here and look forward to meeting you all in person.
